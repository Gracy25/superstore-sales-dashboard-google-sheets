# Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Google Sheets.

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets
- Pivot Tables
- Charts
- Dashboard

## KPIs
- Total Sales: $2,296,957.70
- Total Profit: $286,324.07
- Total Quantity: 37,871

## Key Insights
- West region generated the highest sales.
- Technology had the highest sales.
- Technology generated the highest profit.
- Consumer was the largest customer segment.

- ## Business Insights
- The West region generates the highest revenue, so the business could investigate what is driving its strong performance and apply those strategies in other regions.
- Technology products contribute the most to overall sales, making them a key revenue driver.
- Technology not only generates the highest sales but also the highest profit. This suggests it is both a high-demand and high-margin category.
- The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales. Marketing efforts and customer retention strategies should continue to focus on this segment.
